### CRM Workflow Rules

1. **One reply per 48 hours maximum.** Do not follow up more than once every 48 hours on any single contact.
2. **Follow-up cadence:** Email 1 → wait 5 business days → Email 2 → wait 7 business days → Email 3 → wait 6 weeks → restart cycle.
3. **Named case rule:** If a prospect names a case during any interaction, immediately update the "Named Case" field and escalate to "Pilot Proposed" status. Send the pilot agreement template within 24 hours.
4. **Status escalation:** Only the CEO or designated commercial lead may change status from "Pilot Proposed" to "Pilot Signed."
5. **Closed Lost rule:** A contact is marked "Closed Lost" only after three unanswered follow-ups. Do not mark as Closed Lost after a single non-response.
